✦ AIR SCULPTOR
FINGER TRACKING · 3D SCULPTING IN MID-AIR
🤌
Pinch to Place Bring your index finger and thumb together until they nearly touch. A shape appears at that position in 3D space.
✋
Open Hand to Stop Spread your fingers apart to release. In Tap mode, you must fully open before you can place again.
✏️
Draw Mode — Sculpt Trails Switch to Draw mode to place shapes continuously as you move your pinched hand through the air.
🖱
Orbit & Zoom Drag the scene with your mouse to rotate. Scroll wheel to zoom in and out.
💡 Sensitivity tip: If shapes place accidentally, lower the sensitivity slider. If pinching feels unresponsive, raise it. Good lighting on your hand helps detection.
